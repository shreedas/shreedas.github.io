Short Bio

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Department of Systems Biology, Harvard Medical School. My host is Dr Jeremy Gunawardena. I obtained my PhD from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin under the advisement of Dr Haris Vikalo. Prior to that, I completed my B.Tech in Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur. I was the recipient of the B.C.Roy Memorial Gold Medal for being the best allrounder in IIT Kharagpur, 2007, and was supported by the Microelectronics and Computer Development Fellowship, UT Austin, 2007-2009.
